As We May Think - Vannevar Bush

Bush has made a lots of insights on future technology. The part I love most is that he mentioned on photography where he claimed, "Certainly progress in photography is not going to stop. Faster material and lenses, more automatic cameras, finer-grained sensitive compounds to allow an extension of the minicamera idea, are all imminent". And it certainly come true.

He also forecast that the camera could be so convenient that it could be "within easy reach of his fingers" has also come true which is just like the camera on our phone. I think this is the wild part is how right he was about everyday tech.
