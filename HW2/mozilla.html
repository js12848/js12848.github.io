Reflection — Mozilla Manifesto

The Mozilla Manifesto Addendum feels like a push to remind us that the internet should work for people, not just big companies. It highlights values like privacy, inclusion, and openness, which honestly still feel super important today. It’s less about tech itself and more about making sure the web stays human-centered.
