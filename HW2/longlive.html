Reflection — Long Live the Web (2010)

Tim Berners-Lee’s “Long Live the Web” really shows how important it is to keep the internet open and free. He warns about companies and governments trying to control or limit it, which could ruin the web’s original purpose of sharing and connecting everyone. Reading it now, it feels super relevant—we still deal with the same issues today.

“The Web is critical not merely to the digital revolution but to our continued prosperity—and even our liberty.”
